submersion


A differentiable map f:X→Y differential manifolds X and Y is called a submersion at a point x∈X if the tangent map

T⁢f⁢(x):T⁢X⁢(x)→T⁢Y⁢(f⁢(x))

between the tangent spaces of X and Y at x and f⁢(x) is surjective.

If f is a submersion at every point of X, then f is called a submersion. A submersion f:X→Y is an open mapping, and its image is an open submanifold of Y.

A fibre bundle p:X→B over a manifold B is an example of a submersion.
