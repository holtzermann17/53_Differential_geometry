1. 𝐫⁢(t)=3⁢t⁢i^+t2⁢j^-4⁢t2⁢k^

the first derivative

𝐫′⁢(t)=3⁢i^+2⁢t⁢j^-8⁢t⁢k^

vector magnitude of the derivative

∥𝐫′⁢(t)∥=32+(2⁢t)2+(-8⁢t)2

∥𝐫′⁢(t)∥=9+4⁢t2+16⁢t2=9+20⁢t2

the second derivative

𝐫′′⁢(t)=2⁢j^-8⁢k^

the cross product

𝐫′⁢(t)×𝐫′′⁢(t)=|i^j^k^32⁢t-8⁢t02-8|=(-16⁢t+16⁢t)⁢i^-(-24)⁢j^+6⁢k^

𝐫′⁢(t)×𝐫′′⁢(t)=24⁢j^+6⁢k^

∥𝐫′⁢(t)×𝐫′′⁢(t)∥=576+36=612=2⁢153

∥𝐫′⁢(t)∥3=(9+20⁢t2)3/2

κ⁢(t)=2⁢153(9+20⁢t2)3/2
