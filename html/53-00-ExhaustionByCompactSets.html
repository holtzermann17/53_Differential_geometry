exhaustion by compact sets


Let U be an open set in ℝn (or a manifold with countable base).  Then there exists a sequence of compact sets K1,K2,… such that

Ki ⊆ int⁡Ki+1,i=1,2,…,
U = ∪i=1∞Ki,

where “int” denotes the topological interior.   Such a sequence is called an exhaustion by compact sets for U.
