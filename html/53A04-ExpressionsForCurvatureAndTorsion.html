expressions for curvature and torsion


For a regular (http://planetmath.org/Curve), parameterized curve α:(a,b)→ℝ3, not necessarily unit speed, the curvature κ⁢(t) and torsion τ⁢(t) are given, respectively, by

κ⁢(t) =∥α′⁢(t)×α′′⁢(t)∥∥α′⁢(t)∥3;
τ⁢(t) =(α′⁢(t)×α′′⁢(t))⋅α′′′⁢(t)∥α′⁢(t)×α′′⁢(t)∥2.

References

John McCleary, Geometry from a Differentiable Viewpoint, Cambridge University Press, 1994.
