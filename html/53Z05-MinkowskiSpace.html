Minkowski space


Minkowski space is a 4 dimensional real vector space with a non-degenerate pseudo-metric of signature (-+++).

More precisely, M with a metric g is a Minkowski space iff:

  • •

    M is a 4 dimensional real vector space

  • •

    g is a symmetric 2-covariant tensor (defines a quadratic form )

  • •

    g is non-degenerate (i.e. ∀x∈M,g⁢(x,y)=0⟹y=0)

  • •

    the diagonalization (http://planetmath.org/SylvestersLaw) of g contains one negative and three positive diagonal entries11this convention is sometimes reversed depending on notation.
