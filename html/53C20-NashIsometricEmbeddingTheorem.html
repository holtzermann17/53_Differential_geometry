Nash isometric embedding theorem


Every compact n-dimensional Riemannian manifold M of class Ck (3≤k≤∞) can be Ck-isometrically imbedded in any small portion of a Euclidean space ℝN, where

N=12⁢n⁢(3⁢n+11).

Every non-compact n-dimensional Riemannian manifold M of class Ck (3≤k≤∞) can be Ck-isometrically imbedded in any small portion of a Euclidean space ℝN, where

N=(n+1)⁢12⁢n⁢(3⁢n+11).

The original proof due to Nash relying on an iteration scheme has been considerably simplified. For an overview, see [2].
