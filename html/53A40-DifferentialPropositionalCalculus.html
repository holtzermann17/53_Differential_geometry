differential propositional calculus


A differential propositional calculus is a propositional calculus (http://planetmath.org/PropositionalCalculus) extended by a set of terms for describing aspects of change and difference, for example, processes that take place in a universe of discourse or transformations that map a source universe into a target universe.

3 Formal development
