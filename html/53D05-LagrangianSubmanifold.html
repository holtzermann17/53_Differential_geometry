Lagrangian submanifold


If (M,ω) is a symplectic 2⁢n-manifold (http://planetmath.org/SymplecticManifold), then a submanifold L is called if it is isotropic, and of dimension n. This is the maximal dimension an isotropic submanifold can have, by the non-degeneracy of ω.
